About Me

My passion for computer science dates back to fourth grade, where the Gifted and Talented Education (G.A.T.E.) program introduced me to coding. The programs and games I was able to make in Scratch were mind-blowing and allowed me to express my creativity and indulge in my curiosity. This enthusiasm continued into middle and high school, where I joined the Robotics club and helped out the software team for our robot. After achieving excellent scores on my AP Computer Science exams, I decided I wanted to major in computer science and pursue what I love as a career. Now, with the skills I've practiced in university, I can once again express my creativity through projects like this website, and I look forward to continuing to learn throughout my career.
