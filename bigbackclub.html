About Us

The Big Back Club was founded in 2025 by a group of food enthusiasts who believed that sharing meals is one of the best ways to build connections and friendships. Our mission is to create a welcoming space for everyone to explore diverse cuisines, discover new eateries, and simply enjoy good company.

We organize a variety of food-related social events, from potlucks and restaurant outings to cooking challenges and food-themed game nights. We're more than just a club; we're a family that loves to eat!
